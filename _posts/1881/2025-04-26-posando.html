In "Posturing", Muybridge presents a standing male performing a sequence of controlled poses that simulate muscle tension, stretching, and deliberate body display. Throughout the 32-second duration, the model alternates his arms, rotates his torso, and maintains an upright stance while shifting his body weight slightly.
